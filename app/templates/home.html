Welcome! This site will let you explore the Monthly Adjusted Data provided by the National Transit Database (NTD) of the Federal Transit Administration. Exploring Unlinked Passenger Trips (UPT), Vehicle Revnue Hours (VRH), and Vehicle Revenue Miles by official agency name are supported at this time and you can start your search by clicking on one of the buttons above. All data is a product of the NTD Data Files on their website updated October 2014. Have fun!
